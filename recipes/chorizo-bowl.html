Chorizo Halloumi Bowl

Description

This is a very simple chicken chorizo and halloumi bowl. If you want a quick dinner that is tasty, flavorful and down right delicious this is the recipe for you. You can either serve it over rice or as is for a low-carb option.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of halloumi
  • 2 links of chicken chorizo
  • 1 bunch of spring onions
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • cayenne pepper to taste
  • paprika to taste
  • garlic powder to taste
  • mixed herbs to taste

Steps

  1. Dice up your block of halloumi cheese and add it to a mixing bowl.
  2. Dice up your chorizo and bell pepper and add to same mixing bowl.
  3. Chop your spring onion and add to same mixing bowl.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over the chorizo mix.
  5. Add all the spices and then mix the ingredients in the mixing bowl.
  6. Drizzle olive oil into a hot pan with some minced garlic.
  7. Once the pan is hot, add in the ingredients in your mixing bowl to the pan and stir.
  8. After 5-7 minutes turn off heat, plate, and enjoy!
